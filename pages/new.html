FRAME x FRAME AT BFI SOUTHBANK

  • Aug 12, 2024
  • Oct 1, 2024
  • |
  • London, UK

In partnership with the British Film Institute (BFI), LAIKA is proud to headline the upcoming film season, Stop Motion: Celebrating Hand-Crafted Animation on the Big Screen, this summer at London’s BFI Southbank. Extensive cinematic screenings—including all five LAIKA films—will take place alongside Frame x Frame, an exhibition showcasing LAIKA’s decades of artistry up close and personal. Frame x Frame visitors will get exclusive access to see an array of LAIKA puppets, props, dioramas, and movie artifacts, learn behind-the-scenes stop-motion secrets, and walk through an iconic Coraline set recreated at a human scale.
